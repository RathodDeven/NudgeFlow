# Universal Knowledge Base

## What is NudgeFlow?
NudgeFlow is an automated assistance platform that helps business owners complete their loan applications. We partner with established financial institutions to provide smooth, digital-first loan experiences.

## MSME Loans Overview
- **MSME:** Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises.
- **Purpose:** These loans are typically used for working capital, buying inventory, business expansion, or upgrading equipment.
- **Process:** Most applications are completed digitally through a partner portal.
- **Typical Requirements:** Business KYC (Udyam, Pan), Personal KYC (Aadhaar), and proof of business operations (Electricity bill, GST if applicable).

## Common Terms
- **KYC:** Know Your Customer (identity verification).
- **Offer:** A pre-approved or "reserved" loan amount based on initial data.
- **Disbursement:** The actual transfer of funds to the borrower's bank account.
- **Lender:** The financial institution providing the funds (e.g., Muthoot, ClickPe partners).
- **Portal/Session:** The web link where the user uploads documents and confirms details.
